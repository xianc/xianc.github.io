About

This is a table style, graphical layout design created in early 2012. It plays with textures and patterns to give the design an interesting look. The overall design is small-- 600px wide to be exact-- so although it is not designed to be responsive, with only a few lines of CSS, it can be made to look typically fine on most modern devies.
